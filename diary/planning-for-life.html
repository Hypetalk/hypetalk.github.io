Planning for Life is a Bit Difficult

I'm at the park parking lot trying to figure out if I should go out and walk. I'm exhausted and don't really want to, but I'm forcing myself so I can be active.

I feel like I'm at a place where I just don't want to do anything. Like quitting from life in general. I don't think I have motivation or competition or any sense of another person to help me stay motivated.

I compare myself to a lot of people from my past and those I see on social media — people who have jobs, direction, something to show for themselves. I don't really have something I know what to do with. I want to design stuff, do architecture, but I don't know where to start or how to build a portfolio or do something that feels original.

I want to do something meaningful with my time but I have no idea what I'm really keen or passionate about. I look at things and I'm not sure if I'm actually interested or just going through the motions.

Like when I was writing my thesis — I was honestly pretty scared to encounter anything related to the topic, because I didn't feel like I knew enough or how to properly analyze the conversations I was supposed to be having. That feeling hasn't really gone away.
